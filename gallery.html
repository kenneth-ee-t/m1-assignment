Petone Heritage

Our aim is to sustain the heritage of Petone Borough Council of New Zealand and promote it's attractions.

Membership predominantly consists of former mayors and their retired team, bringing a wealth of experience and insight into local governance. Their tenure in leadership roles equips them with invaluable knowledge and a deep understanding of Petone's history, needs, and aspirations, ensuring effective representation and advocacy on behalf of the community.
